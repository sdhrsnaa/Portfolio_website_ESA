For me, design is the perfect mix of innovation, marketing, and understanding how people behave. College helped me build a solid foundation in design principles, and it sharpened my ability to analyze and solve problems creatively. Interaction design stood out to me because it focuses on understanding users—researching their needs, identifying their pain points, and ultimately finding ways to make their experiences better.
